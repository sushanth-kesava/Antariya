# **App Name**: 

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Role-Based Access: Secure login and registration system for customers, dealers, and admins, providing appropriate access to their respective portals.
- Product Marketplace Browsing & Search: Interactive interface for customers to browse, search, and filter embroidery designs, threads, fabrics, and accessories by category and keywords.
- Product Details & Digital Asset Preview: Detailed product pages showcasing product descriptions, multiple images, and an embedded preview for embroidery design files (DST, PES, JEF, EXP).
- Shopping Cart & Secure Checkout System: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart, manage quantities, and complete purchases through a secure checkout process.
- Dealer Product Upload & Management Interface: A dedicated interface for approved dealers to upload new products (including embroidery design files), manage product listings, and update inventory levels.
- Personalized Order History & Design Downloads: Customers can view their past order history and securely download purchased digital embroidery design files directly from their portal.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendation Tool: An AI tool that suggests relevant embroidery designs, threads, or accessories to customers based on their browsing history and purchase patterns.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep saffron-orange (#D46311) to convey warmth and cultural richness, inspired by traditional Indian crafts.
- Background color: A subtle, warm off-white (#F9F1EF) providing a light canvas that complements the primary color while maintaining visual clarity.
- Accent color: A deep, muted berry/maroon (#981B25) for high-contrast elements, drawing from the requested deep maroon and providing an analogous highlight.
- Headline font: 'Literata' (serif) for an elegant, intellectual, and cultural touch in titles and headers. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, modern readability in product descriptions and general UI text.
- Clean, modern icons with subtle nods to Indian textile motifs or stylized embroidery patterns, utilizing gold accents where appropriate for visual interest.
- A highly interactive, mobile-responsive layout featuring an animated hero section, clean product cards, and smooth hover effects, balancing modern UI with cultural aesthetic.
- Subtle and smooth animations for transitions between pages and interactions within dashboards to enhance user experience without being distracting.
